# <p><a href="https://printersolvy.com/canon-tutorial/"><img src="/canon ij setup.png" alt="canon com ijsetup" /></a></p>
Unpack Your Printer
Remove all packaging materials and protective tapes.

Place the printer on a flat, stable surface.

<h3>2. Power On</h3>
Plug in the power cable.

Press the Power button to turn on the printer.

<h3>3. Install Ink Cartridges & Paper</h3>
Open the cartridge access door.

Insert the Canon ink cartridges securely.

Load plain paper into the input tray.

<h3>4. Connect Your Printer</h3>
USB Setup: Connect the printer to your computer with a USB cable.

Wireless Setup: Use the printer’s control panel to select Wi‑Fi Setup and connect to your network. Canon also offers Easy Wireless Connect mode for quick pairing.

<h3>5. Download Drivers & Software</h3>
Visit <a href="https://github.com/coraharper077/canon-printer-setup/">ij.start.canon</a>.

Enter your printer model.

Download and install the recommended drivers and Canon software package.

<h3>6. Complete Installation
Follow the on‑screen instructions in the installer.

Select your connection type (USB or Wireless).

Print a test page to confirm successful setup.
